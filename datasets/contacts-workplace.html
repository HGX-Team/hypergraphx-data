contacts-workplace

Tags:

Undirected Temporal Social

Description

This data set contains the temporal network of contacts between individuals measured in an office building in France, from June 24 to July 3, 2013. Nodes represent individuals. Original data are pairwise contacts; hyperedges are constructed by promoting cliques of contacts into higher-order group interactions.
