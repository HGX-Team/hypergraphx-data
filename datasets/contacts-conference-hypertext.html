contacts-conference-hypertext

Tags:

Undirected Temporal Social

Description

Temporal face-to-face proximity contacts. Nodes represent individuals. Original data are pairwise contacts; hyperedges are constructed by promoting cliques of contacts into higher-order group interactions.
